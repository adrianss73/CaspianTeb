About Caspian Teb Nedaye Salamat Pars

Caspian Teb Nedaye Salamat Pars is a specialized manufacturer of disposable sterile personal protective equipment (PPE) serving hospitals, clinics, and industrial clients. We produce sterile surgical gowns, disposable surgical packs, shoe covers, caps, and other single‑use protective items designed to reduce cross‑contamination and support safe clinical workflows. Our production lines combine modern non‑woven materials with validated sterilization processes to deliver consistent, reliable products for critical environments.

Mission

To protect healthcare workers and patients by supplying high‑quality, sterile disposable PPE that meets clinical needs and regulatory expectations.

Vision

To be a trusted regional partner for healthcare providers by combining manufacturing excellence, responsive service, and continuous product innovation.

Core Values

  • Quality first: rigorous incoming material checks and in‑process controls.
  • Safety and compliance: validated sterilization and traceable batch records.
  • Customer focus: flexible order sizes and technical support.
  • Sustainability: optimizing material use and responsible waste practices.

Why Choose Caspian Teb

Advantages

  • Sterile, ready‑to‑use products that reduce contamination risk and streamline procedures.
  • Customizable surgical packs tailored to clinical protocols and specialty needs.
  • Robust quality control across raw materials, production, and sterilization steps.
  • Reliable supply and technical support for institutional customers.

What We Do

We design and manufacture pre‑assembled sterile packs and single‑use protective garments that simplify operating room logistics and improve procedural efficiency. Capabilities include custom pack configuration, scalable production runs, and quality documentation to meet procurement requirements.
